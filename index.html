About Prion Medical

A specialist distributor, built around the procedure.

Founded in Belgium and active across Belgium and the Netherlands, Prion Medical partners with leading manufacturers to deliver carefully selected devices for therapeutic endoscopy and interventional ablation. We focus on a small number of technologies and know each of them deeply.

Our Australian operation extends that same model: clinical specialists supported by responsive logistics, training and reimbursement expertise, working directly alongside the gastroenterologists, endoscopists and surgeons who use our devices every day.

  • Focused portfolio

    A deliberately narrow range so our team can offer genuine procedural expertise, not a catalogue.

  • Clinician-led support

    Hands-on case support, structured training and direct lines to our specialists.

  • End-to-end service

    From evaluation and procurement through to reimbursement guidance and after-sales support.

Regulatory & quality

Australian compliance, by design.

Medical devices supplied in Australia are regulated by the Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA). Prion Medical Australia is committed to supplying only devices that are appropriately included in the Australian Register of Therapeutic Goods (ARTG) for their intended use, and to operating in line with applicable Australian regulatory and quality requirements.
